MODE ANALYSIS INTERPRETATION — Stage 10

A. If model school-shares mismatch human shares (significant GOF),
   models do not reproduce the population mixture. WHICH mode they
   concentrate in is the doctrine-vs-practice question: concentration
   in a minority 'textbook' school implies knowledge from written
   doctrine rather than behavioral practice.
B. Context-pull near 1.0 for C_AGG with lower values for C implies
   models defer to authoritative-looking aggregates more than to
   noisy individual examples; negative or >1 values flag overshoot.
C. Systematic signed band errors show where model priors deviate
   from practiced consensus (e.g. over-boosted bass on 'warm').
